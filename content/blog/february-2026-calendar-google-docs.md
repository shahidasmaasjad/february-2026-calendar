*How do I add events to my February 2026 calendar?* To add events, click on the specific date in your calendar. Then, enter the event details, such as the title, time, and any additional notes. You can also set reminders to ensure you don’t miss important events.

*Can I customize the appearance of my February 2026 calendar?* Yes, you can customize the appearance by changing fonts, colors, and adding images. Google Docs offers various formatting options to personalize your calendar to suit your preferences.

*What are the benefits of using a digital calendar over a paper calendar?* Digital calendars are easily accessible, editable, and shareable.
