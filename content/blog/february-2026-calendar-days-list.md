February is the second month of the year in the Gregorian calendar, lasting 28 days in common years and 29 days in leap years. The name ‘February’ comes from the Latin word ‘Februa,’ referring to a Roman festival of purification. Knowing the days in February is crucial for various activities, from personal scheduling to business planning. It differs from other months due to its shorter length and the occasional leap day, which impacts the number of days.

In 2026, February will be a regular month, with 28 days. The presence or absence of a leap day significantly impacts planning. A leap year, which occurs every four years, adds an extra day (February 29th), affecting financial calculations, scheduling, and certain events.
