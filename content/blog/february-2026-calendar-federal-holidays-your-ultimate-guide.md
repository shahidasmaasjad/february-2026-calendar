A calendar serves as a fundamental tool for organizing time, scheduling events, and tracking deadlines. It provides a visual representation of days, weeks, and months, allowing individuals and organizations to plan activities effectively. The inclusion of federal holidays is particularly important as these days often affect business operations, government services, and school schedules. Understanding the calendar and its key dates is essential for seamless coordination and efficient planning.

Federal holidays are designated by the U.S. government and observed nationwide. These holidays typically involve the closure of federal offices, banks, and postal services. Private businesses may also choose to observe these holidays, although it’s not mandatory. Knowing which days are federal holidays is crucial for anyone who needs to interact with government agencies or plan events that might be affected by closures.

In February 2026, the primary federal holiday observed is Presidents’ Day, also known as Washington’s Birthday. This holiday falls on the third Monday of February and commemorates all U.S. presidents, although it is officially in honor of George Washington. It’s a day for reflection on the leadership and history of the United States.

**Q: Is February 2026 a leap year?**

A: No, February 2026 is not a leap year. It has 28 days.

**Q: What is Presidents’ Day?**

A: Presidents’ Day, officially Washington’s Birthday, is a federal holiday observed on the third Monday of February to commemorate all U.S. presidents.

**Q: Are banks closed on Presidents’ Day?**

A: Yes, most banks are closed on Presidents’ Day, as it is a federal holiday.

**Q: Are schools closed on Presidents’ Day?**

A: Many schools are closed on Presidents’ Day, but it can vary by district. Check with your local school for specific information.

**Q: Where can I find a printable February 2026 calendar?**

A: Many websites offer free printable calendars. A simple web search will provide numerous options.

**Q: How can I add federal holidays to my digital calendar?**

A: Most digital calendar applications allow you to add federal holidays automatically through their settings or by importing a holiday calendar file.

**Q: What other notable days are in February besides Presidents’ Day?**

A: Other notable days in February include Groundhog Day (February 2nd) and Valentine’s Day (February 14th), though these are not federal holidays.
