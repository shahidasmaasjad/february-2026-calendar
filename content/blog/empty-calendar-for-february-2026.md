* **Desk Calendars:** These are smaller calendars that sit on a desk, providing a convenient way to view the month.

Each type of calendar is suited for different users. Printable calendars are perfect for those who like to have a physical copy to write on, while digital calendars are great for those who prefer to plan on their computers or mobile devices. Wall calendars are ideal for a central location where everyone can see the schedule, and desk calendars are best for individual use.
