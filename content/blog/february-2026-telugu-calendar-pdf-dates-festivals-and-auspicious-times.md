The Telugu calendar, also known as the Panchangam, is deeply rooted in ancient Indian astronomical calculations. It is based on the position of the moon and the sun, which determines the Tithis (lunar days), Nakshatras (lunar mansions), and Yogams. Unlike the Gregorian calendar, which is purely solar, the lunisolar nature of the Telugu calendar means that months and days can vary in length, often requiring careful tracking. Its relevance extends beyond mere datekeeping; it serves as a guide for significant life events such as weddings, naming ceremonies, and housewarmings, ensuring they are performed during astrologically favorable periods.

The calendar also highlights specific Hindu festivals and fasting days relevant to the Telugu-speaking community.

The exact demarcation of these months is determined by the new moon and full moon cycles. The concept of *Panchangam* is central to the Telugu calendar. It comprises five key elements: *Tithi* (lunar day), *Vara* (weekday), *Nakshatra* (lunar constellation), *Yoga* (astronomical combinations), and *Karana* (half of a Tithi). Each of these elements carries astrological significance and influences the general nature of a particular day. A detailed Telugu calendar PDF will list these for each day of February 2026.

The *Tithi*, or lunar day, is a crucial aspect. It represents the time it takes for the longitude of the moon to increase by 12 degrees relative to the sun. A lunar month typically has 30 Tithis, but because their duration varies, a Gregorian calendar day might experience more than one Tithi, or a Tithi might span across two days. This variation is a key reason why precise calendar references are important.

*Nakshatras*, or lunar mansions, are another vital component. There are 27 Nakshatras in the Vedic system, each spanning 13 degrees and 20 minutes of the ecliptic. The Nakshatra under which a person is born is considered their Janma Nakshatra, influencing their personality and destiny. The daily Nakshatra in the February 2026 Telugu calendar indicates the prevailing celestial influence.

*Muhurtham*, or auspicious timings, are meticulously calculated based on the Panchangam. These timings are considered highly favorable for initiating important activities like starting a business, performing a wedding, or moving into a new home. The February 2026 Telugu calendar PDF will highlight these auspicious windows, helping individuals avoid inauspicious periods known as *Rahu Kalam* and *Gulika Kalam*.
